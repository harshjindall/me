Stoic / Irrational / Sophomore

I am a dilettante software developer and aspiring entrepreneur.

For a brief period, I was at the Centre Point School, Nagpur —During my high school years, I embraced my introverted nature and found solace in my own thoughts and dreams. While my classmates were busy socializing and engaging in various activities, I preferred spending time alone, immersing myself in cricket.

It was during this time that I developed a burning desire to grow. I saw wealth not just as a means to a lavish lifestyle, but as a powerful tool to make a significant impact on the world. Although my introversion posed challenges in networking and building connections, I recognized the importance of stepping outside my comfort zone. I pushed myself to attend school events and engage in conversations, knowing that building a strong network would be crucial in achieving my goals. Throughout high school, I dedicated myself to playing cricket, learning and self-improvement. I devoured books and resources on entrepreneurship, finance, and personal development, equipping myself with the knowledge and skills necessary for success. I knew that a solid foundation was vital in realizing my dreams.

During my higher secondary education, I discovered the power of networking and building relationships. Through these interactions, I expanded my network, learned from experienced individuals, and gained valuable insights into various fields.

I also realized the importance of balance. I understood that being an introvert was an inherent part of my personality, and there were times when I needed to recharge and reflect in solitude. I found ways to incorporate moments of introspection into my extroverted lifestyle, ensuring a healthy equilibrium between social engagement and personal reflection.

While this period, I unexpectedly found motivation and inspiration from the character of Christian Grey. Though he is a fictional character, I was drawn to his unwavering determination and ambitious drive. Christian's ability to navigate complex situations with confidence and assertiveness resonated with me on a deep level.

Currently, I am actively expanding my network and connections, with a dual focus on my side hustle as a stock trader and the upcoming launch of my business venture. Networking plays a pivotal role in both endeavors, as it provides opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals, gain valuable insights, and establish mutually beneficial relationships.

Through participating in online communities, and engaging in local meetups, I am immersing myself in the stock trading and entrepreneurial spheres. By building a strong network, I hope to foster collaborations, learn from experienced professionals, and create meaningful connections that can contribute to the success of both my side hustle and future business venture.
